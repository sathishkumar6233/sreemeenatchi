About Meenatchi Real Estate

Sree Meenatchi Group, encompassing Meenatchi Constructors and Meenatchi Property Promoter, was established in 2010 and draws its name from the revered Tamil deity, Meenakshi Amman. This dynamic enterprise has flourished over 17+ years, achieving an impressive annual turnover of $450,000. Under the visionary leadership of A T Priyanth and Meenatchi Priyanth, the company operates from its expansive 1,800 Sqft office space, employing 16 dedicated full-time professionals and coordinating with over 45 skilled contract workers.
